HOW WE CAN HELP YOU GET TO V. 1.0 AND BEYOND

Getting your Startup from idea to product market fit is a complex task. Therefore we have build both tools and methods to help - not only getting started but also getting to Version 1. and beyond.

When you decide to work with us here is what happens:

Business Goals are being analysed, understood and validated.

You decide to work with us (or not) based on our feedback and your needs.

Together we setup a roadmap dependant on your need (MVP, v.1, v.2). This roadmap includes tech development, customer validations and market research using our tools.

We begin our work together. Within 30-45 days a working prototype is ready or we will not be paid.

Web development
